FAQ

Common questions from owners and flight departments

Do you come to my airport or hangar?

Yes — we're fully mobile and bring our own water, power, and equipment. We regularly work at CMH, Bolton Field, Rickenbacker, and OSU Airport, plus surrounding strips within about 30 nm of Columbus. Farther out is possible for larger jobs — just ask.

What products do you use on polished aluminum and plexiglass?

Only aircraft-rated products. That means pH-neutral aircraft soap, a proper aluminum polish system (no automotive rubbing compound), and acrylic-safe cleaners for windows and canopies — never ammonia-based glass cleaner, which crazes plexiglass over time.

Are you insured to work on my aircraft?

Yes. We carry general liability, products/completed operations coverage, and hangarkeeper's liability, and we can provide a certificate of insurance naming your airport or flight department as additional insured if that's required for ramp access.

Do you offer recurring plans for flight departments?

We do. Many corporate operators put us on a monthly or quarterly schedule so the aircraft is always ready before a trip, and we handle vendor paperwork (W-9, COI, safety questionnaires) up front so it's a one-time setup.

How much does aircraft detailing cost?

It depends on aircraft size, condition, and which services you need — a light single is a very different job from a mid-cabin jet. Send us your aircraft type and a few photos through the quote form and we'll get you an accurate number, usually within one business hour.
